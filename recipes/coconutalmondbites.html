Coconut Almond Bites

Got a sweet tooth? Look no further!

If you're an almond joy fan, these coconut almond bites are sure to impress! And they're quite a bit healthier for you too!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups Unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup Pure maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp Melted coconut oil
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Handfull of raw almonds
  • 1 cup dark meltable chocolate chips

Preperation

  1. Start by placing and blending the coconut, maple syrup, coconut oil, and vanilla extract in a food processor. This should take around 1 - 3 minutes. You want to stop before the mixture turns into butter like consistancy. Better to be slightly "grainy".
  2. Using a spoon, scoop out 1 - 2 tablespoons and roll into a ball. Place on parchment paper and press down gently to flaten the ball out a little at the base while making a small thumb imprint on the top.
  3. Place 1 or 2 almonds within the top thumb print of each "half" ball.
  4. Place into freezer for 15 minutes.
  5. If you're adding chocolate, heat it slowly so that it is melted. Remove coconut almond bites from freezer. Next part is preference; either drizzle, stamp, or even dunk the hald balls into the chocolate.
  6. Once done coating chocolate, place back on parchment paper and let freeze for another 8-12 minutes.
