Grilled Ribeye Steak and Zucchini

Grilled Ribeye Steak and Zucchini is a simple and quick dinner that is guaranteed to satisfy.

My go to quick and easy summer grill out meal. This ribeye steak and zucchini pairing is sure to be enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • 2 8 - 10oz Ribeye steaks (roughly 1-1.5" cuts)
  • 2 Zucchini, large
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • Coarse sea salt

Preperation

  1. Start by slicing the zucchini length wise cuts, not too thin. Place into ziplock bag with soy sauce and let marinate overnight if possible.
  2. Pat dry ribeye steaks. and then lightly coat all sides with 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Apply coarse sea salt liberally all over. I use quite a bit of sea salt, but coat to your preference. Set on plate and let marinate overnight.

Cooking Directions

  1. Let grill warm to medium-high heat.
  2. Place ribeye steaks down for about 12 minutes. Flipping once half way through. Change cooking times as needed dependent on how well you prefer your steak cooked. Try to only flip steak one time.
  3. Set the zucchini on the grill perpindicular to the grill grates for about 10 minutes. This will depend on how thin they were cut. Flip half way through.
  4. Let steaks rest for at least 3 minutes, preferably 5 minutes before cutting.
